About Me

My name is Anxhelo Lushka, I am a 21 year old graphic designer and junior frontend, computer engineering student and a heavy open source user and advocate. I contribute to many FOSS projects, such as Fedora, LibreOffice, OpenStreetMap etc. I am a member of Open Labs Hackerspace, the first hackerspace in Albania, founded in 2012. I am also a Fedora Ambassador and a Document Foundation member.

During the last 3 years I have co-organized many events and conferences such as OSCAL (Open Source Conference Albania) and more.
